GPS

Discover the science behind the Global Positioning System that powers navigation, tracking, and modern communication.

The Core Idea

GPS (Global Positioning System) is a network of satellites that orbit Earth and send signals to receivers on the ground. By calculating the time it takes for signals to travel, GPS can determine your exact position on Earth.
